Our Products

ALL TYPES OF DIES

Every die is engineered to your exact specification — whether you provide a drawing, sample, or just an idea.

INJECTION / COMPRESSION

Plastic Moulding Die

High-precision cavity and core sets for injection & compression moulding. Consistent output, tight tolerances, extended tool life.

SHEET METAL / BLANKING

Punching Die

Heavy-duty dies for sheet metal punching and blanking. Ground to exact specifications for clean, burr-free cuts every time.

V-DIE / U-DIE / WIPE

Bending Die

Custom bending dies for complex profiles. Achieve repeatable bends with minimal spring-back across all metal grades.

DEEP DRAW / PROGRESSIVE

Forming & Drawing Die

Deep drawing and forming dies for complex 3D shapes. Smooth surface finish and accurate profiles for high-volume production.

FLASH REMOVAL / FINISHING

Trimming Die

Precision trimming dies to remove flash and achieve final dimensional accuracy on forged and cast components.

CUSTOM / SPECIAL PURPOSE

Custom Dies

We engineer dies to your drawing or sample — any size, any complexity, delivered on time from our Kanpur facility.
